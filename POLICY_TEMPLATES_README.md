# Policy Templates - Instructions

## Overview

Three blank template files have been created for you to write your own policies:

1. **PRIVACY_POLICY_TEMPLATE.md** - Write your Privacy Policy
2. **TERMS_OF_SERVICE_TEMPLATE.md** - Write your Terms of Service  
3. **REFUND_POLICY_TEMPLATE.md** - Write your Refund & Cancellation Policy

## How to Use These Templates

### Step 1: Open the Template Files
- Open each `.md` file in any text editor
- You'll see section headings and placeholders

### Step 2: Write Your Content
- Replace the placeholder text `[Like This]` with your actual information
- Write your own content for each section
- Add or remove sections as needed

### Step 3: Customize Structure
- Keep the section headings that apply to your business
- Remove sections that don't apply
- Add new sections if needed

### Step 4: Legal Review (Highly Recommended)
- Have a legal professional review your completed policies
- Ensure compliance with local laws and regulations
- Update based on legal advice

### Step 5: Send Back to Developer
- Once completed, send the filled templates back
- The developer will integrate them into the website

## Important Sections to Include

### Privacy Policy Should Cover:
- What information you collect
- How you use the information
- How you protect the information
- User rights
- Contact information

### Terms of Service Should Cover:
- User responsibilities
- Your rights and responsibilities
- Prohibited activities
- Refund policy reference
- Dispute resolution
- Contact information

### Refund Policy Should Cover:
- When refunds are available
- When refunds are NOT available
- How to request refunds
- Processing times
- Contact information

## Tips

1. **Be Clear:** Use simple, understandable language
2. **Be Specific:** Clearly state your policies
3. **Be Honest:** Don't promise what you can't deliver
4. **Be Complete:** Cover all important aspects
5. **Get Legal Help:** Consult with a lawyer for important legal documents

## Questions?

If you need help with structure or have questions, contact the developer or consult with a legal professional.
